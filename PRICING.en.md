## FAQ

### Q: My company makes $2M/year, do I need a paid plan?
**A:** Yes. Community is limited to <$1M revenue. You'll need Evolve or higher.

### Q: Can I use Community for academic research?
**A:** Absolutely! Community is forever free for academic use.

### Q: Can I mix free and paid features?
**A:** Yes. Any paid tier includes all base features.

### Q: What's included in Enterprise customization?
**A:**
- Vertical specialization (crypto, forex, futures)
- Private Docker deployment
- Custom SkillBank library
- Code review + architecture consulting

### Q: Can I use it offline?
**A:**
- Community/Evolve/Pro: Online activation, local execution
- Enterprise: Full offline capability

### Q: How do I upgrade from Community to Pro?
**A:**
1. Visit gcc-evo.dev/pricing
2. Select Pro plan
3. Link GitHub/email account
4. Receive authorization token

### Q: Is there a free trial?
**A:** Pro and Enterprise offer **14-day free trial** (credit card required).
