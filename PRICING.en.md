# GCC-Evo Pricing

See also: [LAYER_STRUCTURE.md](LAYER_STRUCTURE.md) for the authoritative v5.325 free/paid 8-layer directory split.

## Canonical Tier Boundary

- **Community**: UI + L0 Phase 1 + base L1/L2/L3/L5
- **Evolve ($29/month)**: Community + L0 Phase 2-4
- **Pro ($79/month)**: Evolve + full L1/L2/L3
- **Enterprise ($500+/month)**: Pro + L4 + advanced L5 + DA + private deployment

## Tier Table

| Tier | Price | Included | Best For |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| Community | Free Forever | UI + L0 Phase 1 + base L1/L2/L3/L5 | Personal, academic, <$1M revenue |
| Evolve | $29/month | + L0 Phase 2-4 | Teams validating data quality and mathematical modeling |
| Pro | $79/month | + full L1/L2/L3 | Teams needing full memory, retrieval, and distillation |
| Enterprise | $500+/month | + L4 + advanced L5 + DA + private deployment | Large funds and enterprise deployments |

## Notes

- `L4` is not part of the canonical free tier.
- `DA` is not part of the canonical free tier.
- `L0` must distinguish free Phase 1 from paid Phase 2-4.
- Legacy modules may still exist for backward compatibility, but the canonical commercial boundary is defined by `LAYER_STRUCTURE.md`.
