## Signing Process

### Step 1: Review
- Entire CLA carefully
- Consult legal counsel if needed
- Ensure understanding of all obligations

### Step 2: Authorize
- Have legal representative obtain Board/management approval
- Document authorization internally
- Prepare signatory credentials

### Step 3: Execute
Choose signing method:

**Option A: GitHub Bot**
Submit pull request; bot will request Company signatory

**Option B: Email**
Email to baodexiang@hotmail.com with:
- Company name and address
- Legal representative name and title
- Authorized signatory information
- CLA acceptance statement
- Company seal/official signature

**Option C: Physical**
Print and mail signed agreement to:
```
baodexiang
gcc-evo Project
[Address as requested]
```

Submit list of authorized employees to contribute:
- Full names

- Department/team

Update this list as employees join/leave.
